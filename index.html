Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about EmojiArt

❓

Is EmojiArt really free to use?

+

Yes! EmojiArt is 100% free with no hidden charges, subscriptions, or premium tiers. You can create unlimited emoji mosaics without any cost.

🔒

Is my image data safe and private?

+

Absolutely! Your images are processed entirely in your browser and never uploaded to our servers. Everything happens locally on your device, ensuring complete privacy.

📱

Can I use EmojiArt on mobile devices?

+

Yes! EmojiArt works seamlessly on all devices including smartphones, tablets, and desktop computers. The interface is fully responsive and optimized for touch screens.

⚡

How long does it take to generate an emoji mosaic?

+

Generation is lightning fast! Most images are converted to emoji mosaics in just 2-5 seconds, depending on your device's processing power and image size.

🖼️

What image formats are supported?

+

EmojiArt supports all common image formats including JPG, JPEG, PNG, and WEBP. For best results, we recommend using high-quality images with good lighting and contrast.

💎

What resolution is the output image?

+

All emoji mosaics are generated in stunning 2K resolution (2560x1440 pixels), perfect for social media, printing, or using as wallpapers.

✏️

Can I customize the emoji selection?

+

Currently, our AI automatically selects the best emojis based on colors and patterns in your image. Custom emoji selection is coming in a future update!

🚀

Do I need to create an account?

+

No account needed! You can start creating emoji mosaics immediately without any registration, login, or personal information.
